Performer

  1. Repertoire to fill 60-90minutes
  2. Rehearse
  3. Clothing, uniforms or costumes should be appropriate to the style of music
  4. Timelines:
    • Start of organization should at least be 3months
    • Tickets, ads and merchandising – 5weeks
    • Technical requirements – 3weeks
    • Note: Cancelation by the Client – Music Centre may require payment for committed services and cancelation.

Music Centre’s Obligation

  1. To book venue for specified date, if possible
  2. Arrange contracts with Townsville Council, Ticketshop and pay security deposit
  3. To provide Public Liability & Volunteers Personal Accident Insurance for the event
  4. To assist with technical specification
  5. To do advertising thru ticketshop, websites and news media

Promotion of Concert

  1. Provide 300-500 words about the group (history, profile of performers, photos)
  2. The Centre to create posters, flyers, emails at minimum cost. However, the Centre does not have the resources to distribute the posters Television and radio stations will provide free promotion but need at least 3weeks
